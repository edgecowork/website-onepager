Hot Desk

Flexible workspace whenever you need it.

Hot Desks are for workers who need flexible access to workspace, but not necessarily a private office, or even the same desk every day. With the Hot Desk plan you can show up when you want, grab any open seat in the common area, and start working.

As a member of the Edge Network you will get access to exclusive social and professional events and enjoy a wide range of perks and discounts for work and life offered by our partners. Additionally, the Hot Desk plan comes with handling of mail and packages, unlimited phone booth, and two hours each month to use the meeting rooms free of cost.

Hot Desk Part-time

This plan is ideal for those workers who don’t need a desk everyday, but who are looking for something more flexible. With the Part-time plan you get access to all of the same services and benefits as the Full-time plan, but for only half the month. You get 10 working days a month to enjoy all that Edge has to offer.
