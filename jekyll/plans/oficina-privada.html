Private Office
Exclusive work space for you and your team

At Edge we offer private office space for teams of all sizes. Our offices come in a variety of shapes and sizes, making it easy to find something that fits best with your needs. We make it easy to grow your team by removing the logistics of finding a place to work. All of our offices come completely equipped with high quality tables and chairs. Further customization is possible, but must be approved by an Office Manager.

Importante We don’t ask for a guarantee, but you are required to make an upfront deposit of 30% of the first month of rent to reserve your office.

Want to be more active while you work? Equip your office with electronic stand-up desks made by our partner Activadesk. ® Customize the height of your desk and overcome your posture problems once and for all!
