Dedicated Desk
Your permanent desk in a shared private office

Dedicated Desks are for workers who need access to a permanent desk within a shared private office. Permanent means same desk every day, allowing you and your team to leave your monitors and other belongings.

As a member of the Edge Network you will get access to exclusive social and professional events and enjoy a wide range of perks and discounts for work and life offered by our partners. Additionally, the Dedicated Desk plan comes with free printing, handling of mail and packages, a virtual office, unlimited phone booth and four hours each month to use the meeting room without cost.
