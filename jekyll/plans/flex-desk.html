Flex Desk

Ultimate flexibility for mobile workers

Flex Desks are for workers who move across the city A LOT and who need access to workspace in different locations. With this plan you get access to any Hot Desk in the Edge network, allowing you to adjust each day to your changing schedule.

As a member of the Edge Network you will get access to exclusive social and professional events and enjoy a wide range of perks and discounts for work and life offered by our partners. Additionally, your Flex Desk plan comes with free printing, handling of mail and packages, a virtual office, unlimited phone booth and four hours each month to use the meeting room without cost.
