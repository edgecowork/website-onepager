Meeting Room

Kick your meetings up a notch

The meeting room plan is simple. Choose the amount of hours you need each month and then book a meeting room in any Edge location. Book one of our meeting rooms today. Additionally, if you have a meeting room plan, you will get access to all of the benefits and perks that we offer our members.
