Events and Activities

Events play a fundamental role in the Edge experience. We love to use our facilities to host events that teach, inspire and connect.

Workshops, talks, meetups, networking events, game nights, you name it. We are always open to new ideas for events that support and build the local entrepreneurial community. More importantly, we look for events that have a meaningful impact on the people who attend. Even if you are not a member of Edge Cowork, you are more than welcome to use our space to host your event.

Organize your event at Edge

Interested in using our infrastructure to host your next event? No problem. We have got your space needs covered.

Edge has unique event spaces in multiple locations. Host small gatherings and meetups of up to 25 people in El Golf. For larger events of up to 100 people, check out Bellavista and Ochagavía. Each Edge building also boasts meeting rooms for workshops and seminars. We also have round tables that seat up to 8 people for team activities or classes.

All of our event spaces come equipped with audio, microphones, projectors or screens.
