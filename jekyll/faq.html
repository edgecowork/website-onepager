FAQ

¿What is included in an Edge membership?

Members get access to beautifully designed work space with high speed fibre optic internet, custom designed furniture and workstations with built-in power sockets and USB. Enjoy unlimited purified water, tea and roasted coffee. As a member of the Edge Network you will also get access to exclusive social and professional events and enjoy a wide range of perks and discounts for work and life offered by our partners.

¿What types of plans do we offer?

Hot Desk

Hot Desks are for workers who need flexible access to workspace, but not necessarily a private office, or even the same desk every day. With the Hot Desk plan you can show up when you want, grab any open seat in the common area, and start working. Prices vary according to location.

Dedicated Desk

Dedicated Desks are for workers who need access to a permanent desk within a shared private office. Permanent means same desk every day, allowing you and your team to leave your monitors and other belongings. Prices vary according to location.

Private Office

Our private offices come in a variety of shapes and sizes, making it easy to find something that fits best with your needs. All of our offices come completely equipped with high quality tables and chairs. Further customization is possible, but must be approved by a Community Manager.

Corporate

The corporate plan is designed for larger companies to give their employees flexible access to our collaborative work spaces, providing them with a refreshing and unique work environment. This plan is completely flexible. You choose the number of days a month and who on your team has access to our facilities.

Meeting Room

The meeting room plan is simple. Choose the amount of hours you need each month and then book a meeting room in any Edge location. Our meeting rooms come in different sizes and styles, allowing for a wide range of activities, from board meetings to workshops.

¿What time does Edge Cowork open and close?

It depends on the location. Some buildings have access 24/7 access and other are open during business hours.

¿How can I get started working in Edge Cowork?

The first step is to book a tour at any of our locations. You will be received by an Office Manager who will show you the installations and help you get set up to start working immediately.

¿Can I receive guests in Edge?

Of course. You must first register your guest in our application so that they can be checked-in correctly at the reception. It is important to remember that your guests are not allowed to occupy a working desk while they are visiting. You are welcome to meet with your guests in the meeting rooms or lounge areas

¿Can I receive mailing if I work at Edge?

If you have a Private Office, Dedicated Desk or a Virtual Office, you can receive mail and packages. If you only have a Hot Desk, you will need to additionally purchase a Virtual Office to enjoy this benefit.

¿How can I pay?

We accept credit and debit cards in all of our locations. Bank transfers are also accepted.

¿Can I cancel my plan?

If you have a Hot Desk, you can cancel at any time. For Dedicated Desks and Private Offices we require at least one month notice. We also require at least 3 months commitment for Dedicated Desks and 6 months for Private Offices.

¿Can I pay hourly instead of buying a monthly plan?

No. Currently we only offer monthly plans and day passes. If you want to work for just a few hours, you will need to purchase a Day Pass.

¿Can I work in multiple Edge locations?

Only if you have a Flex Desk plan. If you have a Hot Desk plan, you can only use it at the location where you signed up.

¿Is it safe to leave my belongings?

All Private Office and Dedicated Desk areas are locked by key. Additionally we have lockers available for those who have a Hot Desk. Edge Cowork is not responsible for lost, stolen or damaged items.
