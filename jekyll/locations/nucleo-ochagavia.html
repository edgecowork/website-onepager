Edge Ochgavía is located in Nucleo Ochagavía, one of the largest urban recycling and social transformation projects in Chile. For more than 40 years this building was known as the “White Elephant,” the ex. Hospital Ochagavía that was abandoned during the military dictatorship in ‘73 and never finished. In 2016, Megacentro opened the completely renovated hospital as a new business and community center. Nucleo Ochgavía is home to a wide range of companies and services including: Municipality of Pedro Aguirre Cerda, the Civil Registry, health clinic, banks, fitness club, post office, storage units for rent, offices, loading dock for trucks, and much more.
