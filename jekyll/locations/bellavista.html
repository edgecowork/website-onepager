Edge Bellavista is located in the center of Barrio Bellavista, a bohemian neighborhood rich with artists, entertainers and some of Chile's best ethnic and local cuisine. Situated directly in front of Patio Bellavista, you will be working on the doorstep of some of the best eating places in Santiago. Access is easy with connected bike paths and the Metro station Baquedano just a few minutes walk away. Edge Bellavista can be found in a restored colonial house with breathtaking high ceilings and mosaic floors. This house has a lot of history. Prior to Edge, it was known as Bar Constitución, a famous bar.

Edge Bellavista is a single floor that stretches out with various work zones. The first zone includes the reception, coffee bar, and a modular working space that is also used to hold events. The second zone includes meeting rooms, phone booths, Hot Desks and an outdoor patio to get some fresh air or enjoy lunch. The third zone is for private offices.
