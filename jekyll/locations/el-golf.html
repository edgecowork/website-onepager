Edge El Golf is located right in the heart of the Santiago business district between Isidora Goyenechea and Av. El Bosque Norte. Access is easy with two major Metro stations (Tobalaba and El Golf) just a few minutes walk away. The surrounding area is alive with restaurants, banks, shopping centers and the HQ of many large companies. Edge El Golf occupies the first three floors of an old apartment complex which have been completely renovated with an industrial and rustic look and feel. This is our first location and will always be special for that reason.

Edge El Golf has three main sectors. The first floor is the reception and waiting area with coffee bar. The second floor is the Hot Desk area, an open shared workspace with a variety of workstations, including electronic stand-up desks. This floor also includes a lounge to relax or to meet casually with your team and clients. There is also a private phone booth for 1-1 meetings and important calls. The third floor contains Private Offices, a kitchen, a meeting room for 6 people and another phone booth.
